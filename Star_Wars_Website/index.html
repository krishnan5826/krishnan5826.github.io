WELCOME TO THE STAR WARS GAME SITE!!!

Be R2-D2 and rescue all of the orange rebel pilots! Move your arrow keys around the map to move R2-D2. Here is the point system and the rules of the game:

Rebel Pilot: +200 points (catch all of them, you win).

Stormtrooper: -100 points (catch all of them, you lose).

Mynock: +50 points (they look like golden dragonflies).

Tauntaun: +50 points (they look like a T-Rex).

Mouse Droids: +25 points (they are gray and look like small mice).

Puffer Pigs: -25 points (they are brown pigs).
