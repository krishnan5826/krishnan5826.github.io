My Music Video Mash-Up

Background

We had to make a Music Video Mash-Up in Media in eighth grade. A music video mash-up takes clips from other videos, and using editing software, mashes the clips together to make a video. A song is played in the background for extra effect.

I decided to make my mash-up about Real Madrid, because they are one of the best soccer teams in the world, and I admire their style of play. Make sure that your volume is on, and enjoy!
